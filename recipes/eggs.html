Eggs Recipe

Description

Eggs. Eggs are a staple in most kitchens. Everybody has eggs and if they don't, they should. So let's... GET CRACKIN'!!!

Ingredients

  • Eggs, duh
  • Butter
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Butter a hot skillet
  2. Crack and deposit egg into bowl
  3. Whisk egg in bowl to mix it and add air
  4. Pour egg into skillet
  5. Fold egg onto itself constantly
  6. Egg is done when you're done waiting
  7. Add pepper to taste
